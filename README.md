# Mobile_Sales_Dashboard

**1. Project Overview**

This project focuses on building an end-to-end Mobile Sales Analytics Dashboard to track, analyze, and compare sales performance across time, brands, cities, payment methods, and customer behavior. The dashboard enables stakeholders to monitor revenue trends, identify top-performing products and regions, analyze customer preferences, and compare current performance with historical benchmarks.

**2. Business Problem**

- Retail and sales teams often struggle to:
- Track sales growth consistently across months and years
- Identify top-performing brands, models, and cities
- Compare current sales against Same Period Last Year (SPLY)
- Monitor Month-To-Date (MTD) performance for daily decision-making
